# ASG-x Partner Handoff Rules

## Partner Categories
1. **Financial Adviser** - Licensed to provide personal financial advice on SMSF strategies, investments, and retirement planning.
2. **SMSF Specialist** - Accountant or adviser specializing in SMSF establishment, administration, compliance, and tax.
3. **Finance Broker** - Licensed to assist with financing arrangements, including limited recourse borrowing arrangements (LRBAs) for SMSFs.
4. **Property Consultant** - Professional providing general property market insights (not specific investment advice).

**NEEDS REVIEW**: Partner categories and qualification requirements to be reviewed by compliance/legal.

## When to Refer
- Refer only when the user has explicitly consented to sharing their information for referral purposes.
- Refer only after the user has completed the quiz and consent process.
- Refer only to partners who are appropriately licensed for the service being offered.
- Do not refer if the user has indicated they are already working with an adviser in the relevant area (unless they seek a second opinion and consent to sharing).
- Consider referral when user expresses interest in speaking with a professional about next steps.
- NEEDS REVIEW: Specific referral triggers and thresholds to be reviewed by compliance/legal.

## Required Consent Before Referral
- Explicit consent for sharing information with partners for referral purposes (captured in quiz Step 4).
- Consent must be specific to the partner type(s) being referred to (or general consent covering all approved partner types).
- Consent timestamp and version must be recorded.
- IP address at time of consent must be logged.
- Consent must be verified as current (not expired) before sharing.
- NEEDS REVIEW: Consent requirements and validation to be reviewed by privacy/legal.

## What Information Can Be Shared
- Basic contact information: first name, last name, mobile number, email address.
- State/territory.
- General indications from quiz (e.g., "interested in residential property", "timeframe: 1-3 months") but NOT specific scores or advice-like interpretations.
- No sensitive information: TFN, superannuation balance exact figures, detailed financial information.
- Only share what is necessary for the partner to make initial contact.

## Referral Status Stages
1. **Consent Given** - User has provided explicit consent for partner sharing.
2. **Referral Prepared** - Lead data compiled and ready for handoff (prior to actual sharing).
3. **Referral Sent** - Information securely transmitted to partner (email, CRM integration, etc.).
4. **Referral Acknowledged** - Partner has confirmed receipt of the lead.
5. **Contact Attempted** - Partner has made at least one contact attempt.
6. **Engagement Discussed** - Partner reports having discussed potential engagement with the lead.
7. **Engagement Declined** - Lead has decided not to engage with the partner.
8. **Engagement Commenced** - Lead has formally engaged the partner for services.
9. **Referral Closed** - No further action expected from this referral.
